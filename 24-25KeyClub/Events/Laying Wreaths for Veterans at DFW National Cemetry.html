Laying Wreaths for Veterans at DFW National Cemetary(12/14/24)
On December 14th, 2024 Seguin Keyclub volunteered in front of Hobby Lobby from 9am to 7pm where the members rang bells and raised money for The Salvation Army. We thank everyone who came out there to volunteer and for raising the holiday spirit for everyone around them!.
